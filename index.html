Dancing With Demons

Dancing With Demons

Stepping into a room lit by moonlight
through shattered glass and broken mirrors,
He emerges from the darkness
Smile crooked and arm stretched,
tattooed with names of forgotten souls

Her heart flutters and takes his hand in hers
Then a note strikes and she is in his arms
She longingly looks into his eyes of ribboned lies
And lips frosted with empty promises

Resting her head on his chest with an unbeating heart,
She listens to his sweet nothings
Then the music takes an abrupt turn
And he twirls her around, his breath on her neck

She dances and tangos with fate,
Breaking away from him and doing a pirouette
She runs back to him and is lifted up
Seeming like a goddess with the moon carressing her face
as she smiles up at the star-littered sky

He looks up at her with a twisted smile
and eyes that almost admired and pitied her
Her sapphire eyes widen as he lets go of her
And she falls into an endless oblivion
Stretching her arms out to catch the last ray of light.
